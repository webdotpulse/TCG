Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our EV charging solutions, services, and platform.

How long does installation take?
Most residential installations are completed within a few hours. Commercial and multi-unit installations vary depending on the scope of the project, but we typically finish within 1-3 days after planning approval.
Can I integrate it with my solar panels?
Absolutely! Our EMS (Energy Management System) seamlessly integrates with most modern solar inverters. You can configure it to charge your EV using only excess solar power, maximizing your green energy usage.
Do you support billing for company cars?
Yes. Our CPO platform allows you to set up automatic reimbursement for employees charging their company cars at home. The billing is handled automatically at the end of each month.
What chargers are compatible?
Our platform is OCPP 1.6J and 2.0.1 compliant, meaning it works with 99% of modern AC and DC charging stations on the market.
Can I use my own brand?
Yes! We offer comprehensive whitelabel solutions that allow you to launch your own branded EV charging services, including customized mobile apps and chargecards.
