Intelligent Features

Key Benefits of an EMS

We don't just monitor energy; we actively manage it to save you money.

Dynamic Tariffs

Our algorithm watches the EPEX Spot market prices. It charges your battery when energy is cheap (or negative) and discharges it when prices spike.

  • Automated trading
  • Negative price protection

Peak Shaving

Avoid high capacity tariffs. When your consumption approaches your grid limit, the EMS automatically injects battery power to flatten the peak.

  • Lower grid fees
  • Real-time load shedding

Smart EV Charging

Solar-only mode ensures your car runs on sunshine. 'Boost mode' gets you ready quickly if needed, respecting your home's power limits.

  • Priority charging
  • Multi-brand support
Functionality

The Brain of your Household

The controller acts as the central brain, making decisions based on prediction models and real-time data. It optimizes self-consumption, controls your battery dynamically to trade on price differences, and integrates devices via SG Ready.
