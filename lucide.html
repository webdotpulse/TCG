Lucide Icons Documentation

The Charge Grid project uses Lucide icons instead of FontAwesome. Below are examples of how to use them, different sizes, buttons, and boxes.

How to use:

<i data-lucide="icon-name" class="w-6 h-6 text-brand-blue"></i>

Requires lucide.createIcons(); to be called in your script.
