How We Work?

A seamless process from consultation to installation.

01

Determine your needs

The very first thing we do is determine what your specific needs are. Factors such as the type and model of your Electric Vehicle (EV), how often you use the vehicle, the distance you travel daily, play a role here.

02

Choose the type of charging point

There are different types of charging points available, varying in functionality, power and charging speed. It is important to choose a charging point that matches your vehicle and meets your charging needs.

03

Choose the location

The location, accessibility and proximity of the charging point is an important factor. You must take into account factors such as the proximity of the electrical installation and the aesthetic impact.

04

Installation

Once you have chosen a charging point and location, the final step is the installation. We take care of the necessary electrical adjustments and permits, and ensure that the charging point is correctly connected and tested.
